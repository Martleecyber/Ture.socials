About Trump Media

Founded in 2022, Trump Media is spearheaded by Donald Trump, with Truth Social as its flagship platform. Truth Social seeks to offer an alternative to mainstream social media, promoting free speech and a unique perspective. Despite aspirations to become a media powerhouse with multiple platforms, Truth Social encountered challenges, including expulsion from a major platform following the tumultuous events in Denver.
